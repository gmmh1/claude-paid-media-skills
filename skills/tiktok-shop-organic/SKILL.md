## Purpose
Set up TikTok Shop commerce infrastructure (product catalog, affiliate/creator commerce) and grow organic TikTok presence — the free/commerce layer that feeds proven content into paid Spark Ads and drives direct in-app purchases independent of paid media.

## Trigger Conditions
- "Set up TikTok Shop for my products"
- "Set up a TikTok Shop affiliate/creator program"
- "Grow my TikTok account organically"
- "TikTok content calendar / trend strategy"

## Required Inputs
- Product catalog (if setting up Shop) and fulfillment capability
- Current organic account status and follower base
- Content production capacity
- Whether an affiliate/creator commission program is a goal

## Core Capabilities

### TikTok Shop Setup
- Product catalog structure: titles, images, pricing, and category mapping optimized for in-app discovery and shoppable video/livestream tagging
- Shop-linked content: tagging products directly in organic videos and livestreams so viewers can purchase without leaving the app
- Fulfillment and policy compliance basics (shipping, returns) required to maintain good Shop standing

### Creator Affiliate Program
- Setting commission rates and structure to attract creators to organically promote and tag products
- Providing creators with product access/samples and clear (but not overly restrictive) creative guidance, since creator-native content is what makes affiliate programs work
- Distinguishing affiliate/organic creator promotion from paid creator partnerships (`tiktok-ads-spark-ads`/`tiktok-ads-creative-strategy`) — affiliate is commission-based and creator-initiated, paid partnership is a direct booked arrangement

### Organic Content Strategy
- Content pillars matched to the brand/product and platform-native formats (demos, UGC-style reviews, trend participation, behind-the-scenes)
- Posting consistency and native format adherence (vertical, fast-paced, sound-on-friendly since TikTok audio engagement tends to be higher than other short-form platforms) as baseline requirements
- Hashtag strategy as a minor discovery signal, secondary to content quality/completion rate, similar to other short-form platforms

### Discovery & Algorithm Fit
- Completion rate and rewatch rate as TikTok's dominant ranking signals for the For You Page — content structured to reward full viewing (fast pacing, payoff timing, loopable endings) performs best
- Trend/sound participation when genuinely relevant to the brand, avoiding forced trend-jacking that reads as inauthentic

### Organic-to-Paid Pipeline
- Using organic performance data (which videos/hooks got strong completion/engagement) as the primary input for `tiktok-ads-creative-strategy` and as prime candidates for `tiktok-ads-spark-ads` boosting
- This feedback loop is one of TikTok's most valuable practical mechanics for a resource-constrained business — validate cheaply organically before committing paid budget to a new angle

## Workflow
1. Set up TikTok Shop product catalog if commerce is a goal, ensuring accurate product data and fulfillment readiness.
2. Design a creator affiliate program structure if organic-creator-driven sales is a goal.
3. Define organic content pillars matched to brand and platform-native format conventions.
4. Build a sustainable posting cadence, prioritizing completion-rate-optimized structure over volume alone.
5. Identify top-performing organic content as candidates for `tiktok-ads-spark-ads` boosting.

## Outputs
- TikTok Shop catalog setup plan
- Creator affiliate program structure
- Content pillar and cadence recommendation
- Organic performance data feeding paid creative/Spark Ads recommendations

## Rules
- Never recommend forced trend-jacking that doesn't genuinely fit the brand — inauthentic trend participation reads poorly and underperforms.
- Prioritize completion-rate-optimized content structure over sheer posting volume.
- Always route proven organic winners toward `tiktok-ads-spark-ads` consideration before defaulting to fresh paid-only creative production.
